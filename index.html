How to create Twitter account?

  1. Go to twitter homepage and click on the "Sign up now" button.
  2. Begin the sign-up information by providing your username, date of birth, and your choice of an email account or phone number.
  3. Choose how you want to view ads. Click on the button that says "Next."
  4. Click on the button that says "Create Account."
  5. A verification code will be sent to your email address or phone number.
  6. Enter the verification code once you receive it.
  7. Create a password and that should be atleast six characters.
  8. At the top of the settings page, you will see a "photo" tab. Click this and add a photo of you.
  9. Complete the rest of your profile information.And your twitter account is craeted.
  10. Next time you can just click login option by providing your username and password and that directly opens your home page.
